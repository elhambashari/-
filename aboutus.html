About us

All of your conference data will be stored on Swidish servers in an ISO 2700-certified high-security datacenter. This means the data is subject to the very strict data protection standards of the EU's GDPR. Whenever data is transferred, it will be encrypted so that the data is protected from unauthorized access. We never collect or use any of your personal data if we don’t have your permission to do so. That also includes our guarantee that your attendees‘ personal information won’t be used for advertising purposes.

Well before the EU’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), data security used to top the event software requirements lists put together by conference organizers. We’ve always welcomed that since the privacy and security of your data have been one of our top priorities right from the start. That’s why we encrypt all data before transferring and only store information on servers located in Sweden.
