Welcome to Gothenburg IT conference 2024

18 October to 20 October 2024

IMPACT 2024 summit brings together the worlds top data leaders and practitioners for a 3-days event to discover how modern data teams accelerate growth, create new business value, and drive Data + AI trust at scale. These confrences organized by Chalmers University of Technology, covers all aspects of Applications of Surface and Interface Analysis with emphasis on the application of the latest surface-related methods for both fundamental and applied research and technology. Take this opportunity to meet scientists and researchers from academics and industry all over the world.

Gothenburg is a friendly city on the west coast of Sweden with picturesque archipelago islands. It is located in the heart of Scandinavia in between Oslo, Copenhagen and Stockholm and is an excellent place for meetings. Gothenburg is a city with well-known institutions and high-tech companies. We are looking forward to your participation!
